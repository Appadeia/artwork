Linux for open minds

openSUSE® 12.1 is a powerful and free Linux.based operating system for your computer.
Surf the web, work on documents while listening to music, set up a server or desktop software. The choice is yours and you'll have a lot of fun! The openSUSE project, a worldwide community that promotes the use of Linux everywhere, creates this Linux distribution in an open and transparent way as part of the global Free and Open Source Software movement.
Learn more about openSUSE and become a part of the community at 

www.opensuse.org
